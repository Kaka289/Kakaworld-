About KakaWorld

KakaWorld is a global real-time chatting platform where users can safely connect with others based on their preferences. Whether you're looking to chat with someone nearby or across the globe, KakaWorld gives you full control over who you talk to, when, and how.

The platform is founded by Karan Karthik — a 16-year-old visionary from India — along with his AI partner GPT. Together, they built a fast, secure, and exciting social experience with a crow as their symbol of freedom, fun, and flight. 🐦

  • 👫 Gender and Age Filter (16–100+)
  • 📍 Location Filter (Global, Nearby, Countries)
